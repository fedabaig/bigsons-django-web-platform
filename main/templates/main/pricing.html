Pricing & project questions

How does payment work?

To keep things simple and comfortable, projects are usually split into three stages: 30% to start, 40% after you approve the main design, and 30% at launch once the site is ready to go live. For larger or multi-phase projects, BigSons can agree a staged schedule that matches your timeline and comfort level — always agreed in writing before work starts.

Satisfaction guarantee: if, after we’ve delivered the agreed design and used your included revision rounds, you genuinely feel the website does not match what we agreed to build, BigSons will either continue working at no extra cost to make it right or refund your project fee, minus any third-party costs (such as domains or stock photos purchased specifically for your project).

What if I don’t see exactly what I need?

That’s completely normal. These packages are designed as a clear starting point. If your project sits between two options or needs something different, just share a few details and BigSons will prepare a tailored proposal based on your goals, budget, and priorities — not a one-size-fits-all template.
